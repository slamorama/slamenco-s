First Snow.

Mom drives the car and I feel the fancy seat heaters guiding my back along the curves in the fabric.
it's cold and the wind is trying to pull us farther away form home
and I don't know how to pump gas but she doesn't have her glasses
so I push the buttons for her

it is the least I can do
and I change the radio from christmas jazz to U2 to Katy Perry as she tries to stay awake.
